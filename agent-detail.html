About John

Born and raised in the community, John has an understanding of the amazing features and benefits of this area better than almost anybody. He has established himself as an expert in local real estate by helping families and investors find their own personal “American Dream.” Born here in 1968, local resident John is able to advise individuals in regards to the best neighborhoods, schools, job opportunities, and community features. As a licensed real estate agent serving Rancho Palos Verdes, John is proud to be a committed member of the community, giving back wherever he can.
